This is a **demo project** used for testing the Discovery Portal. It is not a real engagement — all content here is placeholder.

<details class="intro-section">
<summary>How to use the portal</summary>

- **Type your response** in the box on each question. It saves automatically.
- **Edit any time.** Come back tomorrow, next week, or after our next meeting and revise. The new version replaces the old one, and a full revision history is kept on every question.
- Click **Mark as answered** when you're done with a question. The button sits below the response field.
- The **comment thread** below each question is for back-and-forth: questions about the question, clarifications, push-back, follow-ups.
- If a question doesn't apply or you don't know yet, leave a comment like *"unknown"* or *"let's discuss"* and we'll take it up live.

</details>

<details class="intro-section">
<summary>About this demo</summary>

The Acme project mirrors the structure of a real discovery engagement (hosting, content, integrations, launch) but with placeholder questions. Use it to exercise comment threads, status toggles, priority flags, and notification preferences without touching client-facing data.

To test as the client role locally, set `DEV_USER_EMAIL=client@acme.test` in your `.dev.vars`.

</details>
